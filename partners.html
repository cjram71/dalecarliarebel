Why sponsor the Rebels?

Your support becomes opportunity.

When you support the Rebels, you are investing in more than a football team. Every partnership can help another player step onto the field.

  • Youth equipment and safe facilities
  • League participation and tournament travel
  • Coaching resources and community events
  • A welcoming environment where different strengths belong

2026 association sponsor

Supported by Falu Energi & Vatten.

Dalecarlia Rebels was selected as one of the local associations supported through Falu Energi & Vatten’s 2026 sponsorship programme, which focuses on movement, community participation and opportunity in the region.
