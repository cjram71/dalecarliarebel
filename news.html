18 July 2026Season update

U18 roadmap confirmed for late July and August

The combined Broncos–Rebels squad is preparing for a three-day training camp at Kungsängens IP, followed by Dukes Tourney in Skövde and the national U18 league opener away against Örebro on 22 August.

28 May 2026Club news

Dalecarlia Rebels receives local association sponsorship

Falu Energi & Vatten selected Dalecarlia Rebels as one of the local associations supported in 2026. The programme supports movement, community participation and opportunities for children, young people and adults.

26 May 2026Youth football

Rebels join forces with Broncos for the 2026 U18 season

Upplands-Bro Broncos and Dalecarlia Rebels are building a combined squad for national competition. The team brings together club-developed athletes, returning players, newcomers, coaches and Rebels participants.

14 February 2026Recruitment

Rebels welcomes new players at Rookie Days

People of all ages were invited to try American football at Kvarnsvedens IP in Borlänge. The introductory session was free, and no previous experience was required.
