Privacy

A simple website with minimal data.

This website is designed to provide public club information without tracking visitors.

What this website does not use

  • No analytics or advertising trackers
  • No cookies
  • No account system
  • No local or session storage
  • No embedded social-media feeds
  • No third-party fonts, scripts or image hotlinks

Enquiry tools

The enquiry tools generate text inside your browser. When you choose “Open email to the club”, your device opens its configured email application using a mailto: link. The website itself does not receive, submit or store the information.

External links

Links to Facebook, Instagram, Falu Energi & Vatten and Upplands-Bro Broncos lead to external websites with their own privacy practices.

Questions about the club or website can be sent to dalecarliarebelsaff@gmail.com.
