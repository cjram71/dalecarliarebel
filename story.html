The Rebel Story

Built from Dalarna.
Built to last.

The club’s history is a story of keeping American football alive through changing generations, player numbers and opportunities.

Timeline

American football in Dalarna did not begin with the Rebels—and it will not end with the current generation.

Before 2012

Before the Rebels

American football had already established roots in Dalarna through earlier clubs, including Falun Copperheads.

2012

A new team

Dalecarlia Rebels was formed after Falun Copperheads ceased operating, helping the sport continue in the region.

The competitive years

Representing Dalarna

The Rebels entered league competition and brought together players from Falun, Borlänge and the wider region.

Rebuilding

When numbers made competition difficult

The club experienced periods when player numbers made regular competition difficult. The work shifted toward keeping knowledge, identity and opportunity alive.

Today

The next chapter

The Rebels is rebuilding through recruitment, youth development, community involvement and renewed opportunities to play—including the 2026 U18 collaboration with Upplands-Bro Broncos.

What survives every season

A role for every strength. Responsibility to the team.

Belonging

Different bodies, backgrounds, abilities and personalities are needed.

Learning

Everyone starts somewhere, and improvement is built one repetition at a time.

Responsibility

Football works only when people protect, communicate and do their job for others.

Resilience

A rebuilding club learns to continue even when the route changes.
