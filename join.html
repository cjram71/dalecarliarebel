Become a Rebel

Your first practice starts here.

You do not need previous experience, specialist equipment or knowledge of the rules. Come as you are. We will introduce you to the team and guide you through the basics.

Tell us you are interested

What to expect

Four simple steps.

  1. 01

    Contact the club

    Tell us your age group, municipality and whether you have played before.

  2. 02

    Get current details

    We will confirm practice time, location and what to bring.

  3. 03

    Meet the team

    Wear comfortable training clothes and football boots or running shoes.

  4. 04

    Try the basics

    Learn movement, ball skills, teamwork and safe technique step by step.

Every strength has a position

What sounds most like you?

Select a strength to explore positions you may enjoy. Coaches will help you try different roles and find the best fit.

Possible positions

Fast & agile

Wide Receiver

Create separation, catch passes and turn space into yards.

Cornerback

React quickly, cover receivers and challenge every throw.

Running Back

Read openings, change direction and accelerate through space.

Safety

See the field, communicate and close ground quickly.

This is only an introduction. Your coaches will help you try several roles.
