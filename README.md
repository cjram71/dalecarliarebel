# Dalecarlia Rebels English website

Static English-language website for `https://dalecarliarebel.com/`.

## Purpose

- recruit youth players, adult beginners and returning athletes
- provide useful parent and equipment information
- explain the 2026 U18 collaboration with Upplands-Bro Broncos
- preserve and strengthen the Rebel history and sponsor proposition
- route English-speaking visitors to the correct club contact
- connect cleanly to the Swedish site at `https://dalecarliarebels.se/`

## Architecture

The former long single-page experience is reorganised into focused pages for Home, Join, Youth & Parents, 2026 Season, Rebel Story, Partners, News, Contact and Privacy.

## Privacy and dependencies

- no analytics, advertising, cookies, storage, external scripts or external fonts
- no third-party image hotlinks
- enquiry tools run locally and open the visitor’s own email app
- no backend or database

## Before publishing

1. Confirm every 2026 date and location with the club.
2. Replace or supplement vector graphics with authorised team photography when durable local files are available.
3. Update the Swedish site with reciprocal page-level equivalents if separate Swedish pages are created.
4. Review all club, insurance and equipment details annually.
